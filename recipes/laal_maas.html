Laal Maas

Laal maas is a spicy lamb curry from Rajasthan.

This is a homestyle curry. Not restaurant style. Slow cooking. Gentle simmering. Tender, flavourful lamb. And a crazy delicious sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Mutton, curry cuts
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • ¼ cups Ghee
  • 2-3 Cloves
  • 10 Black Peppercorns
  • 4 Green Cardamom
  • 2 Black Cardamom
  • 250 gms Onion
  • Salt To Taste
  • 20 Mathania Chilli paste (use soaked chillies)
  • 1½ tbsp Coriander Powder
  • ½ tsp Red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp Cumin Powder
  • 200 gms Curd, beaten
  • 2 tbsp Mustard Oil

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, add mutton, ginger garlic paste and marinate well as keep it aside for 1 hour.
  2. In a large sauce pot, heat ghee, add cloves, black peppercorns, small cardamom, black cardamom let the whole spices splutter.
  3. Add onions and saute till it gets light brown. Add the marinated mutton and saute for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Add mathania chilli paste and saute well for another 8-10 minutes to remove rawness of the chilies. Add salt to taste & mix well. Cover and let it cook for 10-12 minutes on low flame.
  5. In a bowl add curd, coriander powder, red chilli powder, cumin powder, mustard oil and mix well.
  6. Transfer the curd mixture into the pot and stir well. Cover with the lid and cook for 30 minutes on low flame till the mutton becomes tender.
  7. Garnish with coriander sprig and serve hot with bajra roti.
